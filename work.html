StarFruits

Starfruits is a food brand that produces fruits with freeze-dried technology. I joined the project after they have already created the packages in the form of a stand-up pouch bag, and helped them design the retail display box. After I studied the client's needs and requests, I created the box diagrams and box visual design, contacted the overseas printing vendor, and oversaw the printing process. Half a year later, due to product updates, the client requested an optimization of the product packaging for budget reasons. Although freeze-dried products require stringent high-quality packaging with aluminum materials, I have nevertheless successfully updated the product packaging method to reduce cost, and afterward redesigned the related packaging with the product contents.
